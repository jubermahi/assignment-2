Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3-6 years are in the early childhood stage, where they develop crucial social, emotional, and cognitive skills through play and interaction.

Elementary School

Elementary school is a foundational stage of education where children typically aged 6-12 years develop essential academic skills in subjects like reading, writing, math, and science.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle years, typically ages 10-16, are a transformative period marked by significant physical, emotional, and cognitive development. During this time, adolescents navigate the challenges of puberty
